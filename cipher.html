Solstice Cipher

It's the longest day, but the night is closing in. Each round the sun hides a secret cipher. Decode it to push the dark back and keep the light alive.

After each guess you get clues: ● gold = right glyph, right place · ● violet = right glyph, wrong place.
Reach Day 6 — the Solstice to win. The ⚙ Bombe locks one glyph but spends an hour of daylight.
